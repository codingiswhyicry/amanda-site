This is what it says on the tin: I do not. I really enjoyed my work in the mental health space and will continue to write about mental health here, and perhaps work on projects, but I am not going to engage in personal activism or work in the space for the time being. I think mental health is incredibly, incredibly important. I also think it's a space that is very hard to be in for me personally, and I hope you won't take it personally if I don't get involved with your project or ask.
