Seon was an unreleased piece of software designed to be a digital caretaker that enabled people with long-term disabilities to live independently, and to also reduce caregiver burnout. It was a spin-off of a product idea I had for myself, called Juno.

Seon was never released, but I would love for it to have a life in the future, if anyone is interested in a product idea.
