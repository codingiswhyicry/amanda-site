Aetheria was an app released in late 2020 meant to focus on supporting those with more acute and severe mental health problems. This was in response to the mental health field becoming quite crowded for those with general mental health problems (like moderate anxiety, depression, etc). Aetheria was graciously built with the help of a lot of cool people, namely Yifei Ren, Fouzan Alam, Bear Matthews, Ben Henderson, Karan Pandya, and everyone else involved at Astra Labs. Aetheria's branding was donated by [FIND COMPANY].

Aetheria had almost 40+ tools addressing mental health problems underserved by any other software at that time, like Borderline Personality Disorder and Schizophrenia. Aetheria was shut down in 2024 when Astra Labs closed.
