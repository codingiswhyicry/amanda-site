Wow, another great question from the audience. As of writing this in March 7th 2026, I'm not open to this - I have my hands full with Faura and it keeps me more than busy. However, I believe joining companies or being a cofounder is something that should really be taken slowly. Business is a financial marriage, and it's best to get to know all parties involved before jumping in. Try talking to me and spending a bit of time with me before popping the question.

In the event that I would ever cofound or found a company in the future: I prioritize and/or only work with companies that have a consumer or social good angle. I really, more than anything in my heart want to work on things that are for every day people, and make their lifes easier or more fun. I'm not the person to call to cofound a defense startup, or a financial company.
