Oenone is the direct brain child of my best friend, and one of the most compassionate people I know, Devon Price. She's a medical assistant who worked to volunteer with WHATCOM Street Med in Bellingham, Washington. Through that work, she came to me and requested I help build her an application due to the numerous logistical challenges these non-profit groups face when tracking and triaging patients who are often transient and in remote locations.
